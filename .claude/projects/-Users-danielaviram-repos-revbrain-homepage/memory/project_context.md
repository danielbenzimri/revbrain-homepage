RevBrain homepage was started from a copy of geometrix-homepage (a construction SaaS Next.js site). Needs full rebranding for RevBrain - a revenue operations platform for Salesforce Partners. Main product handles revenue migrations from CPQ to RCA. Target audience: partners handling complex migrations.

**Why:** geometrix-homepage provided a stable reference architecture (Next.js 15, Tailwind 4, Contentful, i18n) to accelerate development.

**How to apply:** All content, colors, branding must be changed from construction/Geometrix to revenue operations/RevBrain. Keep the architectural patterns (Contentful mock data, i18n, phase gating).
